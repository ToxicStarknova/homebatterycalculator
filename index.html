2. System & Strategy

Self-Consumption (Solar Maximiser)

Prioritises using your own solar power to reduce grid imports. The battery stores excess solar during the day and discharges to power your home at night. It does not consider electricity prices.

Price Arbitrage (Grid Trader)

Includes all Self-Consumption rules, but also actively trades with the grid. It can charge the battery with cheap grid electricity and sell stored power back to the grid when export prices are high.

Export Maximiser (FIT Maximiser)

Designed for high export tariffs. It force-charges the battery during designated cheap-rate hours, then uses that stored energy to power the home during the day, freeing up 100% of solar generation for export. Use the checkboxes below to set charge/discharge hours.
